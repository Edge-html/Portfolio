DeepLabv3+ for Brain Tumor Segmentation

Role: Testing

Focused on medical imaging precision, this study implements a modified DeepLabv3+ architecture to automate brain tumor boundary analysis. The project compares optimization strategies like Adam and SGD, utilizing Atrous Spatial Pyramid Pooling (ASPP) and Focal Tversky Loss to achieve a significant 140% improvement in Dice Coefficient.

AI-Powered Coral Health Assessment

Role: Testing

This project features a two-stage deep learning framework designed to automate the monitoring of Philippine coral reefs. By integrating a U-Net model for semantic segmentation and EfficientNet-B0 for health classification, the system identifies bleaching with 82% accuracy, providing a scalable solution for conservation.
